## Tropic of Cancer (20260414)
I was wondering how much the latitude of the Tropic of Cancer actually changes through time. I calculated the position of the line through time relative to Mokumanamana. The north to south movement of the tropic is on an ~41,000 year cycle that intersected Mokumanamana recently. The tropic was over the island from 922–959 A.D. The position of the Tropic of Cancer is controlled by Earth's axial tilt (obliquity), which varies over time. Built in R using ggplot2, sf, and plotly. Island outline derived as a KML data polygon from Google Earth. Tropic latitude calculated from an obliquity model.
